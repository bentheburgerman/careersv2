About Us



Are you looking for tasty, juicy burgers in the Edmonton area? Are you craving something more than just your average fast-food burger? Have no worries, The Burger Joint on 23rd Avenue Northwest has what you’re looking for! Using fresh ingredients, our cooks ensure that every bite is perfection in a burger, no matter what kind you order. Getting hungry? Have no worries, The Burger Joint will cook you up something nice. Try one of our tasty signature burgers, like the Hot Tropical Jerk Burger! This sandwich comes topped with jerk chicken breast, guacamole, mayo, grilled pineapple, homemade coleslaw, and banana peppers. Not your style? Maybe our Blue Cheese Burger will hit that craving. This dish comes topped with a seven-ounce beef patty, Italian blue cheese, roasted corn, crispy onion rings, purple onions, lettuce, tomatoes, and drizzled in a blue cheese dressing. Still not what you’re looking for? Don’t worry, The Burger Joint also offers a selection of Build Your Own Burgers! With these burgers, you choose what kind of patty you would like, and then choose whatever toppings you would enjoy on your burger! It’s that easy! Lastly, to finish your meal, grab an order of our delicious Curly Fries, or our Veggie Explosion Salad! No matter the kind of burger you’re in the mood for, The Burger Joint is sure to satisfy those hunger cravings! Come on down to our location, or order online for pickup or delivery through SkipTheDishes, and let us take care of the rest. Don’t wait, order today!
